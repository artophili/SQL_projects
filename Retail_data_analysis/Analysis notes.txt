Four layer of solving SQL
Layer 1 (The Foundation): Get the raw data. ("I need the sales table.")
Layer 2 (The Transformation): Clean or group it. ("I need to turn dates into months and years.")
Layer 3 (The Math): Perform the calculation. ("I need the average sales.")
Layer 4 (The Filter): Apply the final constraint. ("I only want the top one per year.")
